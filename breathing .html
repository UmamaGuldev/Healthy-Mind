What is Breathing Meditation?

Breathing meditation is a simple and powerful technique where you focus on your breath to calm your mind and body. It helps you stay present and reduces overthinking.

By observing each inhale and exhale, you create a sense of inner peace and relaxation. It’s one of the easiest ways to begin meditation for beginners.

Benefits of Breathing Meditation

Reduces Anxiety

Deep breathing calms the nervous system and lowers anxiety levels.

Improves Lung Health

Controlled breathing increases oxygen flow and strengthens lungs.

Boosts Focus

It clears mental clutter and improves concentration.
