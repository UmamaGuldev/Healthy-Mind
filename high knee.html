High Knees

An Effective Aerobic Exercise
for Cardio and Endurance

What Are High Knees?

High Knees is a dynamic aerobic exercise that involves running in place while lifting your knees as high as possible. It is commonly used in warm-ups, cardio workouts, and HIIT routines.

This exercise increases heart rate, improves coordination, strengthens lower body muscles, and helps burn calories quickly.

How to Perform High Knees

Step 1 – Starting Position

Stand straight with your feet hip-width apart and arms at your sides.

Step 2 – Lift Knees

Begin running in place while lifting your knees up toward your chest.

Step 3 – Continue Motion

Keep alternating legs quickly while swinging your arms naturally.

Benefits of High Knees

Improves Cardio Fitness

Boosts heart rate and improves endurance.

Burns Calories

A high-intensity movement that helps burn calories quickly.

Strengthens Legs

Targets quads, hamstrings, calves, and hip flexors.

Better Coordination

Improves balance, speed, and coordination.

Tips for Proper Form

  • Keep your back straight and core engaged.
  • Lift your knees to waist level.
  • Land softly on the balls of your feet.
  • Start slowly and increase speed gradually.
